About Kortec BV

Kortec BV is an innovative technology company specializing in developing advanced software solutions and applying data analytics techniques across various sectors. With a strong focus on the energy sector and applied scientific research, Kortec has previously collaborated with leading organizations such as TNO, Wageningen University & Research (WUR), Alliander, and TenneT.

Our Expertise

Software Engineering

Kortec develops customized software applications tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. From embedded systems to cloud-based platforms, our team of experienced software engineers delivers robust and scalable solutions that form the foundation for innovation across different industries.

Data Engineering and Data Science

With the explosive growth of data, effectively managing and analyzing it is essential. Kortec offers expertise in data engineering and data science, including:

  • Machine Learning (ML): Developing predictive models that provide valuable insights for decision-making.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Processing and understanding human language to enhance communication and interaction.
  • Large Language Models (LLMs): Applying advanced language models for complex task analysis and automation.

Data Warehousing, Data Lakes, and Data Lakehousing

Kortec has extensive experience in designing and implementing data solutions, including:

  • Data Warehousing: Structuring and storing data in a central repository for efficient reporting and analysis.
  • Data Lakes: Storing large volumes of raw data in its original format, enabling flexible and scalable analyses.
  • Data Lakehousing: Combining the benefits of data lakes and data warehouses, allowing both raw and processed data to be effectively managed and analyzed.

Cloud Technologies and Kubernetes

Kortec leverages modern cloud platforms and containerization to provide flexible and scalable solutions:

  • AWS and Azure: Utilizing cloud infrastructures for hosting applications, storing data, and performing analyses.
  • Kubernetes: Orchestrating containers to efficiently scale and manage applications in a cloud environment.

Databricks and MLOps

Kortec utilizes Databricks for developing, training, and deploying machine learning models. This platform supports the entire machine learning lifecycle, from data processing to model deployment. In combination with MLOps practices, Kortec ensures a streamlined and efficient workflow for developing and managing machine learning models.

DevOps and CI/CD

Kortec applies DevOps principles to enhance collaboration between development and operations. By implementing Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, Kortec ensures faster and more reliable delivery of software applications.

Data Modeling and Data Governance

Kortec understands the importance of a structured approach to data management. We offer expertise in:

  • Data Modeling: Creating visual representations of data structures and relationships to improve data quality, facilitate data integration, and enable effective decision-making.
  • Data Governance: Implementing policies, processes, and responsibilities to ensure the accuracy, completeness, reliability, and consistency of data.

Energy and Sustainability

Kortec plays an active role in the energy transition by developing technologies that contribute to sustainable energy solutions. This includes designing energy management systems, integrating renewable energy sources, and improving energy efficiency across various applications.

Applied Scientific Research

Kortec focuses on applied scientific research, utilizing advanced technologies to address complex challenges. This encompasses developing digital twins for precision agriculture, applying artificial intelligence in healthcare, and enhancing food production processes.

Our Mission

At Kortec, we strive to harness technology and data to tackle societal challenges. By developing innovative solutions and collaborating with research institutions, we contribute to a more sustainable and efficient future.
